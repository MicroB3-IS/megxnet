MIBiG Annotation Form

Please consider the following before you start filling out the form:
  • For MIBiG submission of a biosynthetic gene cluster, a GenBank accession number of the gene cluster or genome entry that contains it is required. If your sequence data is private pending publication, you may submit it to GenBank/EMBL/DDBJ with an embargo until the manuscript is out.
  • Please use Firefox/Chrome/Safari/Opera. Some functions may not work optimally in Internet Explorer.
  • Please provide all information in English.
  • Please enter all decimal values using point notation.
  • Make sure you have an active internet connection when you submit the form.
  • Parts of the form indicated with arrows can be folded and unfolded for optimal readability.
  • Some parts of the form are optional, others are mandatory. Mandatory fields are marked by asterisks.
  • When working on a complex submission, we advise to make offline notes in parallel to filling the form, to avoid losing your gathered data if the session is somehow lost or times out.
  • If you have questions while filling out the form, please do contact us. We will try to respond within 24 hours.
