PubMap

The world of publications

PubMap is a search engine designed to find scientific publications by the geographic regions they refer to. It will allow you to access knowledge specific to your geographic area of interest. You can either search for publications by specific geographic coordinates and and radius around them or you choose from a list of regions. Then you will find publications about research going on in this exact area.

Our PubMap database relies on the community, therefore to fill and update our database we need your help. Please install Megxbar and help us with geo-refrencing publications. MegxBar is a very simple to use firefox application for this task. While reading a paper just add longitute and latitude of the samples used in the study and submit it to our database.

PubMap Stats shows an statistical overview about the current top five research areas according to the current PubMap database
