FAQ

When I upload data, why might I want to register to megx.net?

Currently, megx.net is hosting the OSD App Server. Most importantly, it is your data and we would like to correctly link the data to you. We also would like you to log-in in order to maintain security and data quality and avoid misuse of our services. However, for your convenience you can also log-in to megx.net using your Facebook, Google or Twitter account avoiding to create and maintain yet another account.

What happens with my data I send to you?

The data will be stored in our database and made publicly available to everyone via our OSD App Server.

Where can I get more information on how to sample for the OSD?

You can find further information on the OSD webpage http://www.my-osd.org. It hosts tutorials and detailed information about the sampling process.

My GPS does not work! How can I get the geographic coordinates of my sampling point?

Currently, please use an external such map application as e.g. Google Maps. There, you can approximate your geographic location of your data collection with the mouse and do a right click. A little popup menu will appear, select 'What's here?' and the coordinates will appear in the Google search box on top of the website. The first number is the Latitude and the second the Longitude. You can then enter these latitude and longitude values in your OSD App.

Please, if all breaks loose, you can email your data to us. If you have questions and feedback we would be more than happy if you contact us.
