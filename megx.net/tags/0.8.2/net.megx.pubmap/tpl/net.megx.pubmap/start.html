PubMap

The world of publications

PubMap is a search engine designed to find scientific publications by the geographic regions they refer to. Therefore, PubMap will allow you to access knowledge specific to your geographic area of interest.

Our PubMap database content relies on your community input, therefore to fill and update our database we need your help. Please install Megxbar and help us with geo-refrencing publications. MegxBar is a very simple to use firefox application for this task. While reading a paper just add longitute and latitude of the samples used in the study and submit it to our database.
